Top three activities to do in and around Dover

Walk the Battlements of Dover castle!

Spend the day exploring the castle grounds and wartime tunnels beneath.

Have a picnic with the swans at Kearsney Abbey!

Walk along the paths that wind around the lake and maybe stop for a drink at the Billards Room Cafe

Enjoy the view of the white cliffs from Dover sea front!

Sunbathe on the beach and watch as the ferries come in to port. Can you see france across the channel?
